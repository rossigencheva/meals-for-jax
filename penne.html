Penne with Chicken Sausage

Ingredients:

  • Penne pasta
  • Parmesan
  • Rao's marinara sauce
  • Aidell's Italian Style chicken sausages

Directions:

  1. Boil water in a big pot, add salt when it boils.
  2. Cook pasta according to the time on the package.
  3. In a separate pan, place 1/2 an inch of water and heat over medium high until simmering. Add sausages and cook for 10 minutes, adding water when it evaporates.
  4. When pasta is cooked, drain and put back in the pot. Pour the jar of marinara sauce in.
  5. Slice up chicken sausages and add to pot.
  6. Serve with parmesan.
