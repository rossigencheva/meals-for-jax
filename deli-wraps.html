Deli Wraps

Ingredients:

  • Tortilla of choice - Mission Garden Wraps are the best
  • 1 sliced tomato
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Thinly sliced red onion
  • Deli turkey or ham
  • Sliced cheese of choice
  • Mayo
  • Mustard
  • Ranch
  • Pepper

Directions:

  1. Heat big skillet on medium high heat. Once hot, heat tortilla for 15 seconds on each side (this helps to avoid tearing when you're rolling it up later).
  2. Set the tortilla on a plate, put mayo, mustard and pepper in the middle.
  3. Add lettuce, tomato, and onion. Layer on meat and cheese. Top with ranch.
  4. Fold up the sides of the tortilla, then roll until sealed.
  5. Cut in half and serve with chips!
