Italian Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 package garden rotini pasta
  • 1 cup mini pepperonis
  • 1 cup cubed mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup slices black olives
  • 2 cups chopped baby spinach
  • 1/2 container cherubs
  • 1/2 cup Tessemae Green Goddess salad dressing

Directions:

  1. Boil water in a big pot, add salt when it boils. Cook pasta accoring to time on the package.
  2. Once pasta is cooked, place in a big bowl and allow to cool to room temperature.
  3. Add all ingredients to bowl and mix. Cover with salad dressing and mix.
  4. Enjoy!
