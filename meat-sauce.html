Spaghetti and Meat Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 package spaghetti
  • 1 jar Rao's marinara sauce
  • 1 pound ground turkey, beef, or pork
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fennel seeds
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Garlic powder
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan

Directions:

  1. Boil water, add salt, and cook pasta for the amount of time specified on the package.
  2. In the meantime, chop up onion and cook in a skillet with olive oil over medium heat.
  3. When onion is soft, add meat and cook until browned. Add spices and cook another 2 minutes. Pour in marinara sauce.
  4. Once spaghetti is cooked, drain and return to pot. Pour meat sauce into pot and stir.
  5. Serve with parmesan.
