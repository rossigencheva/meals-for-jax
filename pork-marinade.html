Marinated Pork Tenderloin

Ingredients:

  • 1 pork tenderloin (this recipe also works well with pork chops)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 1/2 tablespoon honey
  • Black pepper

Directions:

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a gallon-sized plastic bag.
  2. Add the pork and massage.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, up to 8 hours.
  4. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  5. Remove pork loin from bag and place on baking sheet lined with aluminum foil. Make sure none of the onions or garlic are on the meat or they'll burn.
  6. Bake until center reaches 145 degrees - usually 25-35 minutes. Check with a meat thermometer.
  7. Take out of the oven and place the meat on a cutting board. Cover with foil and rest for 5 minutes before slicing, to keep the juices inside.
  8. Serve with salad or veggies of choice.
