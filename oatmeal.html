Banana Chocolate Oatmeal

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup quick-cooking oats
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tablespoon chocolate chips
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt

Directions:

  1. Pour the oats and the water into a bowl. Add a sprinkle of salt and stir. Cook for 1.5 minutes until fluffy.
  2. Add milk, chocolate chips, and a sprinkle of cinnamon. Stir until chocolate melts.
  3. Slice up the banana and add on top.
