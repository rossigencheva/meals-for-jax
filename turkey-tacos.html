Turkey Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/2 diced onion
  • 8 oz can red enchilada sauce
  • 1/2 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon cumin
  • 1/2 tablespoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil

Directions:

  1. In a pan over medium heat, cook the onion in olive oil for 5-6 minutes.
  2. Add the meat and cook until almost done, 7-8 minutes.
  3. Sprinkle the spices onto the meat and stir to combine.
  4. Add the enchilada sauce and cook for another 5 minutes until it reduces a bit.
  5. Serve with tortillas or lettuce wraps. Add whatever toppings you like!
